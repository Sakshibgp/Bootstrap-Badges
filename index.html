Credit cards can now be linked to UPI, starting with RuPay: RBI

The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) on wednesday announced that credit cards will now be allowed to be linked with the UPI accounts. The implementation will begin with the indigenous RuPay credit cards, followed by other card networks such as Visa and Mastercard. Untill now, customers could only link their debit cards to UPI.
